A rock festival in a city with methodical people, had the following disposition of people at the ticket booths:

Window 1 -> 1
Window 2 -> 2 3 4
Window 3 -> 5 6 7 8 9
Window 4 -> 10 11 12 13 14 15 16
Window 5 -> 17 18 19 20 21 22 23 24 25

Window N -> (...) X (...)

Note that the number 1 was written in the first line and in the following lines there are two more numbers than in the previous line.

It is necessary to identify the counter (N) where person number X purchased the ticket. develop a function that generates this value.
